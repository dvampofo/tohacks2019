About the Application

Have you ever wondered if it is worth to buy TTC MetroPass in terms of how many times you travel in a month? We have the same concern as you, so we built this web application to help you decide if you need to start buying or stop buying the TTC MetroPass. Simply go to your Presto account and download your travel record. We will make your money more valueable.
